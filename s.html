Materials Layout

PLYWOOD

BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) and BWP (Boiling Water Proof). Both grades offer superior durability, strength, and moisture resistance.

HDHMR

HDHMR (High-Density High Moisture Resistance) board is a moisture-resistant wood product known for its superior strength and durability.

EXT - MDF

Exterior MDF is specifically designed for outdoor or high-moisture environments. It is treated with water-resistant resins and additives to prevent swelling, warping, and degradation when exposed to moisture.

MDF

MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) is commonly used in furniture, cabinetry, and interior applications due to its smooth surface and ease of machining.
